Loads the game bundle directly into this page and invokes its registered factory with a mocked bridge. Bypasses the widget iframe (the widget requires https for game-src).

For production-style testing through the real widget iframe, use host.html from a context that serves over HTTPS (jsDelivr, a Cloudflare Pages preview, a local mkcert-backed tunnel, etc.). The direct-mount harness here exists for fast game-DOM iteration.
